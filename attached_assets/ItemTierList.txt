# Echo Realm — Complete Item Tier List

> **Scoring note for armor/weapons:** Tiers are assigned by combat effectiveness.  
> Armor uses a weighted score: `maxHp + (DEF × 8)` — DEF is weighted heavily because it reduces every hit for the whole battle.  
> Weapon rankings are purely by ATK value.  
> Proc effects are graded separately and factored into combo rankings.
